NayeJaisa - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 March 2026

NayeJaisa (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the NayeJaisa mobile application, a platform for buying and selling second-hand mobile phones in India. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, phone number, delivery address, and support messages. We also collect precise location data (with your permission) to show nearby sellers and enable faster delivery services. Additionally, we store order details, account information, and device-related information necessary to operate the app.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used to create and manage your account, verify users, process orders, arrange delivery, provide customer support, improve app performance, prevent fraud, and enhance overall service quality.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with delivery partners, payment providers, cloud service providers (such as Firebase), and legal authorities when required by law or to complete your orders.

4. Location Data

Location data is collected only with your permission. It is used to show nearby products, improve delivery speed, and enhance user experience. You may disable location access at any time through your device settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using trusted cloud infrastructure such as Firebase. We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You may update certain profile information within the app. You may request account deletion or data removal by contacting support through the app. Upon verified request, we will delete your data in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Children’s Privacy

NayeJaisa is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted within the app and on our official Privacy Policy page.
